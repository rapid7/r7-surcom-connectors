# __Description__
  Connector for Proactivanet

# __Overview__

  Proactivanet is a powerful and adaptable IT service and asset management solution, recognized by the industry and the users themselves as a benchmark in the sector.

  This connector synchronizes device, mobile and PC details, and key relationships, from Proactivanet into the Rapid7 platform.

# __Documentation__

  This connector requires `Base URL`, `API Key` with read access to (CIs related to DBMS, PCs, Domain, Location and Users) and `Accept-Language`.
